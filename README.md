## 🧠 About Me  

Cybersecurity and networking learner driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and the goal of understanding how systems behave, break, and get defended.  

I enjoy exploring security from a blue team perspective — reading logs, finding patterns, understanding attacks, and turning raw events into meaningful investigation stories.  

My focus is on building practical SOC and security monitoring skills through hands-on labs, real-world scenarios, and continuous learning.
